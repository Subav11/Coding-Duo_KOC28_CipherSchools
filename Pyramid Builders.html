About Us

Pyramid Builders was started in 2021 as a partnership firm by Subham Yadav, Akshat Yadav & Others. In its aspiration of creating a “HOME FOR EVERYONE”, Pyramid Builders is engaged in Property Development mainly of Promotion of residential spaces in Punjab. Pyramid Builders is known for its excellence in quality of Homes with a delivery on time and without cost escalations, along with necessary completion certificates from the regulatory authorities. Pyramid Builders gives guarantee for its homes and its transparency in area calculation and provides maximum plinth area.
